1. Data Scientist

Reference Number: DS105

Position Title: Data Scientist

Brief Description: The Data Scientist will collect, process, and analyze large datasets to uncover insights that guide strategic decisions and optimize company performance.

Salary Range: $85,000 to $115,000 per year

Reports To: Head of Data Analytics

Key Responsibilities

  • Gather, clean, and analyze structured and unstructured data.
  • Develop predictive and statistical models to support business objectives.
  • Visualize data findings through dashboards and reports.
  • Collaborate with engineers and analysts to ensure data quality and accessibility.
  • Communicate technical insights to non-technical stakeholders.

Required Qualifications, Skills, Knowledge, and Attributes

Essential
  • Bachelor’s degree in Data Science, Statistics, Computer Science, or related field.
  • 3+ years of experience in data analysis or predictive modeling.
  • Proficiency in Python, SQL, and libraries such as pandas, NumPy, and scikit-learn.
  • Strong knowledge of data visualization tools (Tableau, Power BI, or Matplotlib).
  • Excellent analytical and communication skills.
Preferable
  • Master’s degree in Data Science or related discipline.
  • Experience with big data tools (Hadoop, Spark).
  • Knowledge of cloud data environments (AWS, Azure, GCP).

2. AI Engineer

Reference Number: AI203

Position Title: AI Engineer

Brief Description: The AI Engineer designs, builds, and deploys artificial intelligence systems to automate business processes and enhance data-driven innovation.

Salary Range: $90,000 to $120,000 per year

Reports To: Head of Data Science

Key Responsibilities

  • Design and implement machine learning and deep learning models.
  • Collaborate with data scientists and developers to deploy AI solutions.
  • Train, evaluate, and optimize algorithms for accuracy and performance.
  • Integrate AI models into production systems and monitor performance.
  • Stay updated on emerging AI technologies and frameworks.

Required Qualifications, Skills, Knowledge, and Attributes

Essential
  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, AI, or a related field.
  • 3+ years of experience in AI or machine learning development.
  • Proficiency in Python and ML frameworks (TensorFlow, PyTorch).
  • Strong understanding of algorithms, data structures, and neural networks.
  • Excellent problem-solving and teamwork skills.
Preferable
  • Master’s or PhD in Artificial Intelligence or Machine Learning.
  • Experience with cloud AI tools (AWS SageMaker, Google Vertex AI).
  • Familiarity with NLP, computer vision, or reinforcement learning.
